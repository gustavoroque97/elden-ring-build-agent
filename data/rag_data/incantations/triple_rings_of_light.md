# Triple Rings Of Light
**Item Type:** Incantation

**Description:**
One of the incantations of the Golden Order fundamentalists. Produces three rings of light and fires them forwards. The rings of light return to a position close to the caster before disappearing. This incantation can be cast repeatedly. A gift from the young Ivliquella to his father, Radagon.

**Cost:**
23

**Slots:**
1

**Effects:**
Fire three rings of light before the caster
