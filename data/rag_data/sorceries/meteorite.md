# Meteorite
**Item Type:** Sorcerie

**Description:**
A glintstone sorcery that draws on gravitational force, used by a great white king with stone skin. Calls small meteorites down from the sky. The meteor shower continues for as long as the sorcery is in effect. It is said that, in the Eternal City, now lost in ruin underground, meteorites held the same import as stars.

**Cost:**
30

**Slots:**
1

**Effects:**
Calls small meteors down from the sky.
