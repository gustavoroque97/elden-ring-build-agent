# Ash Of War: Storm Wall
**Item Type:** Ashe

**Description:**
This Ash of War grants no affinity to an armament, but imparts the following skill:"Storm Wall: Swing the shield to create a wall of storm winds in front of you, deflecting arrows and other such physical projectiles. Can also be used in the same way as a regular parry."Usable on small and medium shields.

**Affinity:**
Standard

**Skill:**
Storm Wall
