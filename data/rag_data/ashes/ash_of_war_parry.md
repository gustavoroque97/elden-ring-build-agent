# Ash Of War: Parry
**Item Type:** Ashe

**Description:**
This Ash of War grants no affinity to an armament, but imparts the following skill:"Parry: Use this skill in time with a foe's melee attack to deflect it and break that foe's stance. This provides an opening to perform a critical hit."Usable on daggers, curved swords, thrusting swords, fists, claws, and small and medium shields.

**Affinity:**
Standard

**Skill:**
Parry Skill
