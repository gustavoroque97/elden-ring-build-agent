# Ash Of War: Shield Crash
**Item Type:** Ashe

**Description:**
This Ash of War grants no affinity to an armament, but imparts the following skill:"Shield Crash: Two-hand the shield and charge forwards while maintaining guard. Weaker enemies will be shoved backwards and can even be staggered. Hold to extend the duration of the charge forwards."Usable on all shields.

**Affinity:**
Standard

**Skill:**
Shield Crash
