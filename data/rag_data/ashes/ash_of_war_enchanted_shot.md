# Ash Of War: Enchanted Shot
**Item Type:** Ashe

**Description:**
This ash of war grants no affinity to an armament, but imparts the following skill:"Enchanted Shot: Archery skill that enlivens the arrow with spiritual essence. The resulting shot will fly faster than regular shots and change its trajectory to follow the target."Usable on light bows and longbows.

**Affinity:**
Standard

**Skill:**
Enchanted Shot
