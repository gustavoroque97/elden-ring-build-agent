# Incantation Scarab
**Item Type:** Armor
**Category:** Helm | **Weight:** 5.1

**Description:**
Golden scarab worn directly on the head. These scarabs roll clumps of incantations during their labors. Slightly reduces the FP costs of incantations, but increases damage taken. As a scarab approaches death, it abandons its rolled treasure and stretches its wings wide for long journey to its home nest.

- Strike | amount: -5
- Slash | amount: -5

- Holy | amount: -5
