# Land Squirt Ashes
**Item Type:** Spirit
**FP Cost:** 0

**Description:**
Ashen remains in which spirits yet dwell. Use to summon the spirits of three land squirts Summoning consumes HP rather than FP. These spirits appear some distance from the summoner. The firm, fleshy tubes of the creatures remain rooted to the spot, spraying poisonous liquid over their surroundings

**Effect:**
Summons three land squirts

240
