# Banished Knight Engvall Ashes
**Item Type:** Spirit
**FP Cost:** 100

**Description:**
Ashen remains in which spirits yet dwell. Use to summon the spirit of Engvall, the Banished Knight. One of the two knights dubbed the Wings of the Storm. Despite his banishment, he rejected the invitation of the Grace-Given Lord, instead keeping watch over a masterless castle for many years, gaining renown as a hero of the fringes.
