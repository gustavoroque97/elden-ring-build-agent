# Avionette Soldier Ashes
**Item Type:** Spirit
**FP Cost:** 67

**Description:**
Ashen remains in which spirits yet dwell. Use to summon the spirits of two avionette soldiers. Spirits of marionette soldiers with avian features that were created to serve a sorcerer. Equipped with long-hafted scythes, they also attack from the skies by lobbing fire pots. Can sometimes malfunction when damaged.

**Effect:**
Summon the spirits of two avionette soldiers
